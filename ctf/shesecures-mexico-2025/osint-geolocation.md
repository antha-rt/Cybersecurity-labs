# OSINT / Geolocation

## Objective

Reconstruct the route of a historical expedition using fragmented textual records containing mathematical expressions and partial location clues.

---

## Approach

Solved equations provided in the dataset to derive numerical coordinates.

Interpreted the results as geographic latitude and longitude values.

Plotted the coordinates using mapping software.

Identified the geographic location corresponding to the derived coordinates.

---

## Result

The identified location corresponded to the key required to complete the challenge.

---

## Skills demonstrated

- Analytical reasoning

- Coordinate reconstruction

- OSINT geolocation techniques

- Data interpretation
