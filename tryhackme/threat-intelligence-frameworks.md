# Threat Intelligence and Attack Frameworks

This section explores frameworks used to understand adversary behavior and structure cyber attack analysis.

## Cyber Kill Chain

Introduces the traditional Cyber Kill Chain framework used to analyze intrusion stages:

- Reconnaissance
- Weaponization
- Delivery
- Exploitation
- Installation
- Command & Control
- Actions on Objectives

Skills developed:
- Recognizing attacker activity phases
- Understanding intrusion progression
- Identifying opportunities to disrupt attacks

---

## Unified Kill Chain

Expands on traditional models by integrating additional attack stages and aligning with modern frameworks such as MITRE ATT&CK.

Skills developed:
- Attack lifecycle analysis
- Understanding attacker motivations and tactics
- Applying structured frameworks for defensive analysis

---

## Introduction to Cyber Threat Intelligence

Explores the fundamentals of CTI and its role in SOC operations.

Topics covered:

- Threat intelligence lifecycle
- Indicators of compromise (IOCs)
- Intelligence enrichment
- Intelligence sharing platforms

Focus:
Applying threat intelligence to alert triage and incident investigation.
